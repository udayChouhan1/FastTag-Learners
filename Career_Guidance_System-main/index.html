Find an Internship

An internship can help you start your career by gaining practical work experience. Doing an intern helps you apply your learning in the real world, get familiar with the work environment, enhance your resume and even boost your confidence. Apart from these, the most valuable thing that internships provide is the opportunity to build your network. A popular saying in the professional world is that your network is your net worth. And as a new graduate, having a network with industry professionals means a better chance of landing your dream job. To find an internship, you may check vacancies on a potential employer's website, send cold emails to companies, attend career fairs or go through online job portals.

MBA

You can pursue Masters in Business Administration (MBA) to build your career in management or turn your technical knowledge into a successful business. Pursuing an MBA degree can benefit BTech graduates in many other ways. For instance, studying MBA can help develop your managerial skills, start your own company and land higher posts in an organisation. There are also a lot of MBA specialisations that you can pursue. For instance, you can pursue an MBA in sales, marketing, human resource, finance, operations, banking & financial services and business analytics. To apply for MBA, you typically need to crack the entrance exam recognised by the colleges you wish to apply to. While there are a number of such exams, the Common Admission Test (CAT) is accepted by most of the leading management institutes.

Higher Studies

You can enrol in a post-graduation programme after your BTech. It is an excellent path for students looking to enhance their subject expertise and gain specialised knowledge. Getting a master's degree can also help you get senior-level jobs and earn a higher salary. Some of the common MTech specialisations that students can opt for include: software engineering electrical engineering computer science and engineering power systems engineering mechanical engineering thermal engineering chemical engineering machine design To apply for MTech, you must have a valid score in the respective entrance exams accepted by the colleges. The primary national-level entrance exam accepted by the top institutes like the Indian Institutes of Technology ( IITs), National Institutes of Technology (NITs) and Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs) is the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE). Several colleges also accept state-level entrance exams such as Post Graduate Common Entrance Test (PGCET).

Civil Services Entrance Exam

If you aspire to work in the government sector, serve the country and want a job with good pay and perks, then UPSC can be the ideal choice. To join Civil Service, you have to clear the UPSC Civil Services Exam. Following that, you are eligible to apply for over twenty government services including the Indian Administrative Service (IAS), Indian Foreign Service (IFS), Indian Revenue Service (IRS) and the Indian Police Service (IPS) based on your score. The UPSC also conducts exams specifically for engineering graduates, such as the Engineering Services Examination (ESE) and the Combined Engineering Services (CES) examination. Clearing these exams makes you eligible for varied government engineering positions. Choose which UPSC exams you want to sit, depending on your interest and career goals. If you are interested in general public services, then Civil Services is the ideal option. If you are inclined to engineering and technical jobs, then Engineering Services is the way to go.

Study Abroad

Studying abroad provides you with a world-class education and international exposure. You also get to choose diverse options of colleges and courses, expanding your scope of educational opportunities. In fact, graduates with international degrees are highly valued in the global market, making them more employable. And if you have plans of settling abroad, then studying in certain countries can improve your prospect of getting permanent residence. Many students pursue studies in a foreign country, get a work permit there and then apply for permanent residence. To study abroad, you have to meet the necessary criteria of the college and the country's immigration policies. You may want to visit the college website to check the available programs, course details, requirements, admission procedure and important dates. Many colleges even provide counselling to assist international students with the admission process.
