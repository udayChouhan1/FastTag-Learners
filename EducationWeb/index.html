Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for courses?

To register for courses, log in to your student portal and navigate to the registration section. Select the courses you wish to enroll in and follow the prompts to complete the registration process.

What are the tuition fees and payment options?

Tuition fees vary depending on the program and course load. Payment options typically include credit card, bank transfer, and installment plans.

Can I transfer credits from another institution?

Yes, we accept transfer credits from accredited institutions. To transfer credits, submit official transcripts from your previous institution(s) for evaluation.

What academic programs and degrees are offered?

We offer a variety of academic programs and degrees at the undergraduate and graduate levels. Visit our academic programs page on our website to view the full list of programs offered..

How do I request transcripts?

To request transcripts, log in to your student portal and navigate to the transcripts section. Follow the instructions to request an official transcript to be sent to the desired recipient.

What is the academic calendar?

The academic calendar includes important dates such as the start and end of each semester, holidays, and exam periods.
